Her Story

Art born from survival

Tosha Hanvey is a survivor. Her journey through abuse, recovery, and healing is the foundation of every brushstroke she makes. Art became her language when words weren't enough.

"When I paint, I paint for the person who feels exactly how I used to feel. You are not alone."

Tosha has shared her story at events for fellow survivors, speaking with raw honesty and the courage that only comes from having truly walked through fire. Each piece is created to meet you exactly where you are, and remind you that you are enough.
